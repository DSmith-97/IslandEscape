*Island Escape Jet Ski and Boat rental customers are not permitted to go out to the ocean*

General

  • Please arrive 30 minutes early.
  • We will need sufficient time to instruct the driver and go over safety rules.
  • If you are late, you are cutting into your rental time.

Anyone born after 1/1/88 needs a boating license to operate a jet ski or boat.

Get your Florida boater education temporary certificate issued by an authorized vendor online: www.boat-ed.com/floridarental/

View the boater safety video here: Boater Safety Video (YT)

We offer a credit/reschedule option valid within 6 months of the booking date.

We require 5 days notice for a full refund. If you are canceling less than 5 days before your reservation, you will have the option to reschedule.

  • Valid government issued photo I.D.‘s for anyone wishing to drive
  • One credit card per reservation.


Jet Ski

  • Persons 18 or older may operate a jet ski.
  • Persons 16 and 17 years of age must have guardian/parent’s permission to operate a jet ski by themselves.
  • Persons 15 years of age may drive with an adult as a passenger.

  • Jet Ski
  • Life Jackets
  • Dry storage pack for your phone, wallet, personal items..
  • Fuel
  • Passengers ride FREE

A $300 hold will be placed on your credit card and released once the unit is returned and inspected.

Even though the jet skis are rated as a three person ski, you will be more comfortable with a maximum of 2 adults and 1 child.

  • There is a small compartment for a lunch box-sized cooler or other personal items.
  • We cannot guarantee that your items will not get wet.
